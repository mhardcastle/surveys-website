For referees

This site gives referees access to publications and data products from the LOFAR surveys KSP.

These data and publications are shared with you in confidence and you must not distribute them or allow others to access them through the login credentials that you have been given.

  • Paper III: Host-Galaxy Identifications and Value-added Catalogues (Kondapally et al)
  • Paper IV: Photometric Redshifts and Stellar Masses (Duncan et al)
  • The bright-end of the infrared luminosity functions and the abundance of hyperluminous infrared galaxies (Wang et al)
  • On the contribution of discrete sources to the sky temperature at 144 MHz (Hardcastle et al)
