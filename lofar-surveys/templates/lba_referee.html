LBA survey of HETDEX: Referees page

This page provides referee access to the LBA survey of HETDEX, as a single mosaic over the HETDEX field and associated catalogue. 25,247 sources are detected and the rms noise level is around 5 mJy/beam at a resolution of 45 arcsec and an effective frequency of 55 MHz.
