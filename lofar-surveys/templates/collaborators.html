For collaborators

This site gives preliminary internal access to data products from the LOFAR surveys KSP.

You must not use the catalogues or images provided here in publications without first discussing your proposed project with the relevant working groups and getting it approved through the KSP. You must not share the images, catalogues or preview links outside the collaboration without discussion with surveys project management.

For further information on the data releases please see the LOFAR Surveys KSP wiki, in particular:

  • DR1 data release description
  • DR1 optical ID catalogue description
  • Publication policies
  • List of approved science projects.

Data products available

  • DR1 HETDEX
  • DR2 preliminary
  • Dynamical spectra from DR2
  • Deep fields
  • Legacy datasets
