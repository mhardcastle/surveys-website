For collaborators

This site gives internal access to data products from the LOFAR surveys KSP.

You must not use the catalogues or images provided here in publications without first discussing your proposed project with the relevant working groups and getting it approved through the KSP. You must not share the images, catalogues or preview links outside the collaboration without discussion with surveys project management.

The latest KSP publication policy is here.

For further information on the data releases please see the LOFAR Surveys KSP wiki.

Data products available

  • DR1 HETDEX
  • DR2 internal release
  • LoTSS-wide raw fields
  • Dynamical spectra from wide fields
  • LoTSS deep fields internal release
  • Euclid Deep Field North internal release
  • GAMA fields internal release
  • H-ATLAS NGP internal release
  • LBA HETDEX survey preliminary
  • Legacy datasets
