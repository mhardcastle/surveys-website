Deep fields

This pages provides KSP access to the radio data for the three Northern-sky deep fields (Boötes, Lockman Hole and ELAIS-N1).

All data have been flux-corrected and PyBDSF catalogues have been generated. For general science we recommend you use the radio images in the centre of the field (full-resolution, blanked at 0.3 PB, cropped) but other images are available if needed.

Optical catalogues will be generated at a later stage. If you're interested in helping please contact Martin to sign up to the Zooniverse project (you must be logged in to Zooniverse and have been given access for this link to work).
