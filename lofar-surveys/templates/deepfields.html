LoTSS Deep fields DR1

This page provides public access to the first data release of the LoTSS Deep Fields. These are deep LOFAR pointings in three well-studied extragalactic fields: Elais-N1 (16:11:00 +55:00:00), Lockman Hole (10:47:00 +58:05:00), and Boötes (14:32:00 +34:30:00). Each pointing covers around 20 square degrees, reaching rms depths in the central regions of about 19, 25 and 36 μJy/bm, respectively. The data release also includes multi-wavelength data over the central sky areas, together with source-associated and cross-matched catalogues over the central ~10 square degree region of each field with the highest quality multi-wavelength data. The radio datasets for Bootes and Lockman are described by Tasse et al. (2021) and Elais-N1 is described by Sabater et al. (2021). The multi-wavelength data, source association and cross-matching are described by Kondapally et al. (2021), with corresponding photometric redshifts described by Duncan et al. (2021).
