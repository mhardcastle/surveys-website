Wide fields: internal data release

This page gives preliminary internal access to the wide fields imaged as part of LoTSS. You can search all available fields for an object or a position and view pipeline products for individual fields. Note that if your area of interest is in the DR2 area, you should check the DR2 pages first.

Please note that these fields are not at the full quality of the final data release. They include a wide area and your target source may be well down the LOFAR beam. In addition, the flux scale is not necessarily correct (the 'scale' column in the table gives an estimate of the scale correction that we believe should be applied for a given field.
