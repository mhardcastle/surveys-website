DR2: internal data release

This page gives preliminary internal access to the data for the second LoTSS data release (DR2). You can preview the images through a HIPS viewer, search all available fields for an object or a position, or download full-quality DR2 mosaics and catalogues.

DR2 is expected to consist of two discrete fields, denoted the 0h and 13h fields, covering 5720 square degrees in total.
