DR2: internal data release

This page gives preliminary internal access to the data for the second LoTSS data release (DR2). You can preview the images through a HIPS viewer, search all available fields for an object or a position, download full-quality DR2 mosaics and catalogues or pipeline products for individual fields.

DR2 is expected to consist of four discrete fields, denoted the 0h, 8h, 13h and NGP fields.
