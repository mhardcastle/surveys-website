H-ATLAS NGP field

This page provides KSP access to the LOFAR survey of the H-ATLAS NGP (4th generation: for earlier generations see the Legacy pages). The data are largely the DR2 data in the NGP area, supplemented by a few fields on the LoTSS grid outside DR2 at the southern edge of the field and by the three Cycle 1 pointings described by Hardcastle et al (2016). We present these as a single large mosaic (38000 x 36000, 10 GB) matched to the H-ATLAS NGP field and covering the full area surveyed with Herschel. The rms noise is variable across the mosaic but reaches 50 microJy/beam in the best regions.

These images were made using the DR2 pipeline by Martin Hardcastle. Anyone wishing to use them for science is free to do so but should inform Martin as well as the DR2 infrastructure list.

No optical crossmatching has been carried out on these data as yet, but the DR2 sky will be covered by standard DR2 cross-matching, and we anticipate that a DR2-type optical catalogue will be produced. Any cross-matched catalogue produced by others should be shared with the KSP.
