Boötes and H-ATLAS internal data release

This page gives preliminary access to data products from the Boötes and H-ATLAS NGP surveys and test data from factor/ddf-pipeline development.

Catalogues available

  • Boötes
  • H-ATLAS (2016 version)
  • H-ATLAS (2017 version)
  • P219+52: factor, ddf-pipeline, ddf-pipeline matching factor
(For Boötes and H-ATLAS (2016) these are the associated catalogues from the survey papers. They do not contain optical identifications — discuss those with the authors if needed. For P219+52 and H-ATLAS (2017) they are the raw source lists from PyBDSM.)

Images available

  • Boötes: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • H-ATLAS NW: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • H-ATLAS NE: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • H-ATLAS SW: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • H-ATLAS Central: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • H-ATLAS Mosaic (2017): beam corrected, rms map
  • P219+52 factor: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
  • P219+52 ddf-pipeline: beam corrected, uncorrected, rms map
